Meet Your Coach

Hello, I'm Shannon

"Healing didn't come from forcing it — it came from learning how to listen to my body."

After my divorce, I remember feeling exhausted and lost, unsure how to keep moving forward when my heart felt shattered. On the outside I was functioning. On the inside, my body was bracing, holding its breath, trying to survive what it had just lost.

I did not need more advice. I needed my body to feel safe again. Through my own healing, I discovered that what truly helped was not pushing harder or thinking my way through the pain, but learning how to work with my nervous system.

Today, I support women around the world who are navigating heartbreak or divorce and want a different way to heal — one that doesn't rush the process, suppress emotions, or keep them stuck.

Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure if this is right for you? These are the most common questions from women just like you.

Start Your Journey
I've tried therapy, books, and healing before — how is this different? +
This work begins with the body, not just the mind. Rather than talking about the pain endlessly, we use somatic and nervous system based tools to help your body actually feel safe again. Many women find this approach offers relief they haven't found elsewhere — because it addresses the physiological root of heartbreak, not just the emotional story.
I don't have much time — will I be able to do this? +
Absolutely. The practices are designed to be short and accessible — many just 5 to 15 minutes. You can do them at any time: during a lunch break, before bed, or even in a parked car. Healing doesn't require hours; it requires intention and consistency over time.
What if I'm still in contact with my ex or not fully ready to move on? +
This work meets you exactly where you are. You don't need to be "ready" in any particular way. Whether you're still in contact with your ex, co-parenting, or simply unsure of what you want — the practices here support your nervous system and wellbeing regardless of your relationship status.
What kind of support will I get if I need help? +
Shannon and her team are available within 24 hours to support you. There is a risk-free 24-hour guarantee — if you're not happy with your purchase, you can request a full refund, no questions asked. You are never alone in this process.
