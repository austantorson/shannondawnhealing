Meet Your Coach

Hello, I’m Shannon

“Healing didn’t come from forcing it — it came from learning how to listen to my body.”

After my divorce, I remember feeling exhausted and lost, unsure how to keep moving forward when my heart felt shattered. On the outside I was functioning. On the inside, my body was bracing, holding its breath, trying to survive what it had just lost.

I did not need more advice. I needed my body to feel safe again. Through my own healing, I discovered that what truly helped was not pushing harder or thinking my way through the pain, but learning how to work with my nervous system.

Today, I support women around the world who are navigating heartbreak or divorce and want a different way to heal — one that doesn’t rush the process, suppress emotions, or keep them stuck.

Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure if this is right for you? These are the most common questions from women just like you.

Start Your Journey
I’ve tried therapy, books, and healing before — how is this different?+
This work begins with the body, not just the mind. We use somatic and nervous system based tools to help your body actually feel safe again. Many women find this approach offers relief they haven’t found elsewhere.
I don’t have much time — will I be able to do this?+
Absolutely. The practices are designed to be short and accessible — many just 5 to 15 minutes. You can do them at any time: during a lunch break, before bed, or even in a parked car.
What if I’m still in contact with my ex or not fully ready to move on?+
This work meets you exactly where you are. You don’t need to be “ready” in any particular way. The practices support your nervous system regardless of your relationship status.
What kind of support will I get if I need help?+
Shannon and her team are available within 24 hours to support you. There is a risk-free 24-hour guarantee — if you’re not happy with your purchase, you can request a full refund, no questions asked.

📖 How to Publish Your Site
Follow these steps every time you want changes to go live on all devices.
Why Export & Upload?

Changes you make here save to this browser on this computer only. GitHub is the real website everyone in the world sees. Export & Download bakes all your changes into index.html — upload that to GitHub to publish worldwide.

Step-by-Step
1
Make changes in the CMS
Edit text, images, links in the sidebar. Changes auto-save and update live as you type.
2
Click 💾 Save
Commits all fields to browser storage. Confirmation appears at the bottom.
3
Click ⇓ Export & Download
Downloads index.html with all changes baked in. This is the file you upload to GitHub.
4
GitHub → index.html → ✏ Edit
Open your repo, click index.html, click the pencil icon, Select All (Ctrl+A), Delete, then paste your new code.
5
Commit changes → Wait 2-3 min → Live ✦
Click the green Commit changes button. GitHub rebuilds automatically. Your changes go live on every device worldwide.
